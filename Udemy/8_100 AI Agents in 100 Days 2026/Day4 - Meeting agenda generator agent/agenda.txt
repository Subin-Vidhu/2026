Title: Q1 Product Planning
Objective: Decide priorities for Q1 roadmap and align teams
Duration: 60 minutes

   Owner: Product Lead

2. Feature Review (10 min)
   Owner: Product Lead
   Outcome: All potential features for Q1 are reviewed and understood by stakeholders.

3. Discussion & Alignment (20 min)
   Owner: Product Lead
   Outcome: Consensus reached on trade-offs, technical feasibility, and market impact.

4. Decision: Select Top 3 Features (15 min)
   Owner: Product Lead
   Outcome: Top 3 features are formally decided and approved for Q1 development.

5. Action Items & Next Steps (10 min)
   Owner: Product Lead
